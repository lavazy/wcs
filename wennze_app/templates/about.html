Who We Are

Wennze Cloud Services (WCS) is a proudly African ICT and Managed Services Provider (MSP) founded in 2020. Born out of a desire to bring reliable, cutting-edge technology to the African continent, WCS exists to bridge the digital divide and fuel economic growth through innovation.

From our humble beginnings, we've grown into a dynamic, forward-thinking company focused on delivering tailored technology solutions for individuals, small businesses, enterprises, and public sector organizations. Our work is guided by a core belief: Africa deserves world-class digital infrastructure and solutions that are both accessible and sustainable.
